SpaceX Falcon 9 Rocket Launches Exploration

During this project, I predicted if a Falcon 9 Stage 1 Rocket would launch and land successfully. SpaceX advertises Falcon 9 rocket launches on its website with a cost of 62 million dollars; other providers cost upward of 165 million dollars each. Much of those savings is because SpaceX can reuse the first stage. I present the findings here to showcase the likelihood that the Stage 1 will land. This project was hosted by IBM Skills Network as the Capstone Project for the IBM Data Professional Certificate.
